Our Story

Founded in 2010, marshallairline Aviation emerged from a vision to revolutionize aviation services in Kenya and East Africa. What began as a small helicopter charter operation has grown into the region's most trusted aviation service provider.

Our journey started with a single helicopter and an unwavering commitment to safety and excellence. Today, we operate a modern fleet of aircraft, serving diverse clients from safari tourists to business executives, emergency services to cargo operations.

With over 500 safe flights and counting, we've built our reputation on reliability, professionalism, and exceptional customer service. Every flight we operate reflects our core values of safety, excellence, and innovation.
