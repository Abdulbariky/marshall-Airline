Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance for regular charter flights. However, we can accommodate same-day bookings subject to aircraft availability. For emergency services, we respond immediately.

What's included in the charter price?

Our charter prices include the aircraft, pilot, fuel, insurance, and basic refreshments. Airport fees, overnight crew expenses (if applicable), and catering upgrades are quoted separately.

What are the weight and baggage limitations?

Baggage limits vary by aircraft type. Generally, helicopters allow 15-20kg per passenger, while private jets have more generous allowances. We'll specify exact limits when you receive your quote.

Can flights be cancelled due to weather?

Yes, safety is our priority. Flights may be cancelled or delayed due to adverse weather conditions. We monitor weather continuously and will notify you of any changes as early as possible.
