Professional Aircraft Maintenance

Our certified maintenance team provides comprehensive aircraft maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) services, ensuring your aircraft operates at peak performance and safety standards.

With state-of-the-art facilities at Wilson Airport and a team of experienced technicians, we handle everything from routine inspections to major overhauls. Our maintenance services comply with Kenya Civil Aviation Authority (KCAA) regulations and international aviation standards.

Our Maintenance Services

Comprehensive aircraft maintenance solutions for optimal performance and safety

Routine Inspections

Regular pre-flight, post-flight, and scheduled inspections to ensure aircraft airworthiness and identify potential issues before they become problems.

  • Pre-flight inspections
  • Daily/weekly checks
  • Progressive inspections
  • Annual inspections

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance based on flight hours, calendar time, or cycles to prevent failures and extend aircraft service life.

  • Engine maintenance
  • Component replacement
  • Lubrication services
  • Systems testing

Corrective Maintenance

Expert repair services for unscheduled maintenance issues, component failures, and damage repairs with minimal aircraft downtime.

  • Emergency repairs
  • Component rebuilding
  • Structural repairs
  • Troubleshooting

Major Overhauls

Complete aircraft overhauls including engine rebuilds, major component replacement, and comprehensive refurbishment services.

  • Engine overhauls
  • Transmission service
  • Avionics upgrades
  • Interior refurbishment

Airworthiness Management

Complete airworthiness management including compliance tracking, documentation, and certification maintenance.

  • Certificate management
  • Compliance tracking
  • Documentation control
  • Regulatory support

Parts & Logistics

Comprehensive parts management and logistics services with access to a global network of suppliers and certified components.

  • Parts procurement
  • Inventory management
  • Exchange programs
  • Warranty support
